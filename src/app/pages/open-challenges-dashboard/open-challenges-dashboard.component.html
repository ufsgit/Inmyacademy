Challenge Overview

Challenge: Storytelling Challenge

Create a 1-2 minute video sharing your original tale using sketches, props, or pure narration. Showcase your creativity and storytelling skills to captivate the audience.

Submission Rules

  • • Video format: MP4, MOV, or WebM (minimum 1080p)
  • • Duration: 1-2 minutes
  • • Clear audio required (minimum 128kbps)
  • • Original content only

Judging Criteria

  • • Story quality and originality (40%)
  • • Presentation and production (30%)
  • • Engagement and impact (30%)

Support Lift Rules

  • • Each £1 support = 1 lift
  • • Maximum 20 counted lifts per supporter per entry
  • • Lifts contribute 20% to final score

Prizes

  • • 1st Place: £1,000 + Trophy + Global Recognition
  • • 2nd Place: £500 + Certificate
  • • 3rd Place: £250 + Certificate
