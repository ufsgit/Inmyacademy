How Scoring Works

Merit-first scoring with community support

Understanding Lift Support

After approval, you'll get a personal shareable link and QR code. Supporters can send a £1 Lift to cheer you on, adding +1 to your community score per Lift. Your core project quality drives 65% of the score, making Lifts a helpful but secondary element.

Project Merit

65%

Assessed for overall quality, originality, clarity, and relevance.

Community Support

35%

Your Lifts, normalized against the category's top count for equity.

Score Calculation

Final Score = (Merit Score × 0.65) + (Normalized Lifts × 0.35)
Example A:

90/100 merit with 850 Lifts (category max 1500) = 79 points

Example B:

84/100 with max Lifts = 88.6 points

Emphasizing strong ideas over sheer popularity.

Purpose of the £1 Entry Fee

This small fee verifies your submission, covers prize pools and certificates, and maintains a level playing field. It never influences your score.
