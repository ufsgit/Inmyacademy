Scoring System

Merit-first scoring with community support

Lift Support (Community Boost)

After your Phase 1 submission is approved, you'll receive a unique Lift link and QR code. Families and friends can Send a Lift (£1) to support your project — each Lift adds +1 to your Community Support score.

Project quality still matters most.

Project Merit

65%
  • Creativity & Originality
  • Clarity of presentation
  • Alignment with the task
  • Overall quality

Community Support

35%

Your Lifts, normalised against the top count in your category for equity.

Score Calculation

Final Score = (Merit Score × 0.65) + (Normalized Lifts × 0.35)

Example A:

90/100 merit with 850 Lifts (category max 1500) = 79 points

Example B:

84/100 with max Lifts = 88.6 points
